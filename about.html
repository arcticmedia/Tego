About Us

Tego IP Attorneys is a full service IP law firm in Iceland that commits to provide its clients with the relevant expertise that matches their demands.

"The best lawyer is not the one who wins the most cases, but the one who prevents the most problems."

Our team of experienced IP attorneys specializes in intellectual property law, offering comprehensive legal services including trademark registration, patent protection, design rights, and domain name disputes. We work closely with businesses and individuals to safeguard their intellectual assets and ensure their innovations are properly protected under Icelandic and international law.

With over a decade of experience in the field, we have successfully represented clients across various industries, from startups to established corporations. Our commitment to excellence and attention to detail has made us a trusted partner for IP protection in Iceland and beyond.
