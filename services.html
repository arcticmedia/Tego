Our Services

Tego places emphasis on ensuring that clients receive services from specialized staff according to the needs of each specific project. Our comprehensive range of intellectual property services is designed to protect and maximize the value of your innovations and creative assets.

Trademarks

Comprehensive trademark registration and protection services to safeguard your brand identity and intellectual property rights. We handle trademark registration, search & clearance, opposition & cancellation, renewal & maintenance, and international trademark protection.

Designs

Protect your creative designs and industrial designs with our specialized design registration and enforcement services. Our expertise includes design registration, search & analysis, infringement protection, portfolio management, and international design protection.

Domains

Domain name registration, management, and dispute resolution services to protect your online presence. We provide domain registration, dispute resolution, portfolio management, cybersquatting protection, and domain transfer services.

EP Validations

European Patent validation services to ensure your patents are properly protected across European jurisdictions. Our services include EP validation strategy, national phase entry, translation services, validation deadline management, and post-validation support.

IPR Management

Comprehensive intellectual property rights management to maximize the value of your IP portfolio. We offer IP portfolio strategy, audits & due diligence, valuation & assessment, risk management, and IP commercialization services.

Assignments & Recordals

Professional handling of IP assignments, transfers, and recordals to ensure proper ownership documentation. Our services include IP assignment agreements, change of ownership recordals, merger & acquisition support, license agreement drafting, and chain of title verification.

Counterfeit

Anti-counterfeiting strategies and enforcement to protect your brand from unauthorized use and imitation. We provide counterfeit detection, customs recordation, enforcement actions, market monitoring, and anti-counterfeiting strategy development.

Other Services

Additional specialized legal services to meet your unique business and legal requirements. Our expertise extends to patent services, copyright protection, trade secret protection, IP litigation support, and legal consulting.
